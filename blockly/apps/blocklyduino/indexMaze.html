Welcome to ArduRookies Maze!

My name is Byte, and together we will learn how to code our way out of this Maze!

Do you think you can do it?

Try to use JoyStick blocks, Action blocks and an if-do block to code the JoyStick. Think what should happen if you move the JoyStick in a specific direction. When you think you are done, press "Run the game", and help me escape!

Avoid the enemies and collect the treasures in your way to escape!

Watch out for the sounds! Adding different blocks results in different sounds.

Press Ok/Enter to continue.

Use the blocks to program the JoyStick.
